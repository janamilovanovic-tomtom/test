Motivation

With regards to Audio, there are two types of system setups need to be mentioned. First one is when lane level vehicle positioning is provided, and second when it is not. Let call it lane localisation further on. Lane localisation allow for more precise an contextual audio guidance since lane position of the vehicle is known. When Lane GPS positioning is provided by the system, more precise audio guidance can be enabled. The purpose of Audio LLG is to nudge driver towards correct lane/s without reliance on the screen.
